# SaloneHealth Connect (DPG Project)

## Problem
Limited access to healthcare in rural Sierra Leone due to long distances, shortage of medical professionals, and lack of reliable health information.

## Solution
SaloneHealth Connect is a digital platform that connects users with nearby health workers, provides essential health information, and supports SMS-based consultations for areas with limited internet access.

## Target Users
- Rural communities
- Pregnant women
- Youth and families
- Community health workers

## SDG Alignment
- SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being
- SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure
- SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities
